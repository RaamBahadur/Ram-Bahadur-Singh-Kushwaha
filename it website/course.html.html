BHM

We provide bachelors degree in Bachelor Of Hotel Management.BHM or Bachelor of Hotel Management course is a 4-year undergraduate degree in hospitality management.The BHM program recognizes the knowledge and skills required for various jobs in the hotel and catering industry. The programme course structure is classified into four main components; the compulsory courses, the core courses,industrial exposure and project work, and the elective courses.

BBA

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) aims at educating students and preparing them for handling managerial positions to start a professional career in business or public management. It will enable them to take over responsible positions in domestic and global business organizations with modern impersonal skills and technical know-how of the recent trends in global business.

MBA

A master of business administration (MBA) is a graduate degree that provides theoretical and practical training for business or investment management. An MBA is designed to help graduates gain a better understanding of general business management functions.Most major universities and colleges provide MBA programs, which usually last two years.
